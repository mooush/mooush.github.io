Consultations

Your initial visit

Your initial visit to our clinic will be for a consultation, which will last approximately one hour. During this consultation our Consultant Trichologist will discuss any concerns you may have, ask you questions about your medical history, allergies, medication, lifestyle, haircare routines etc.

In some cases, we may need to investigate further to give you an accurate diagnosis, correspond with your GP for blood tests, or treatments to eliminate any possible underlying health conditions. Our Consultant Trichologist will give you an accurate diagnosis, along with possible treatment options, and an honest prognosis of your situation.

What is Trichology?

Trichology is a medical term for the study of the hair and scalp derived from the Greek words Trikhos (Hair) and LOJIK'L (Doctrine of). A Consultant Trichologist is considered the best place to go if you are suffering from hair loss and scalp conditions, as they are considered experts in their field.

Our Consultant Trichologist can assist you by competently diagnosing, manage and treat hair and scalp issues such as; Hair loss, Hair thinning, Hair breakage, Hair shaft defects, Excessive scalp oiliness, Scalp itching, Afro hair - Traction hair loss from hair trends like; weaving, extensions or scalp braids, Androgen-related hair loss in both men and women. Scalp diseases that can cause irritation. Inflammation dryness or redness of the scalp such as Psoriasis, Dandruff, Eczema and Pityriasis.
