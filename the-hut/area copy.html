the Hut

Our goal was to build an amazing hut that we ourselves would want to stay in if we went on holiday. We made it as ecologically perfect as possible with respect to it's surroundings and future inhabitants. The Hut is set in 2 hectares of cork and oak forest. since we have been on the land we have also been busy reforesting and setting up a fruit forest garden. Your stay will be nothing short of a complete immersion into nature without foregoing any of city life's luxuries. A space to unwind, explore and recharge.
