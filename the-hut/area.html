the area

Situated in the hills surrounding the historic town of Lousã and mountains of Serra de Lousã and Serra de Açor, the Hut is a perfect base to explore central Portugal.

The area is well known for it’s ancient and picturesque slate villages (Aldeias do Xisto) and lush river beaches. The beiras region is ideal for outdoor pursuits. There are ample tracks for mountain biking, a huge network of stunning nature walks, and activities like canoeing, motorcycling and wild swimming. We will provide a treasure map with the best of them.
