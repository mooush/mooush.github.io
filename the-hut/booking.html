Booking

The Hut sleeps two people in a double bed. A cot is available on request. We also have a bell tent available for rent which sleeps two people on single mattresses and is ideal for older kids.

The Hut has:

  • Private compost loo with a view
  • Secluded outdoor bathroom
  • Covered outdoor kitchen/eating space.
  • All the basic cooking equipment.
  • A small solar 12v system which provides lighting
  • Full solar charging of phones / tablets (USB socket).
  • Wi-fi can be accessed from a shaded arbor near by.
